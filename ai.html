About

Background & tools

I spend most of my time in the space between design and engineering — sketching interfaces, then building the real thing myself so nothing gets lost in translation.

Before going independent, I led front-end for two early-stage product teams in Stockholm and Berlin. I care about interfaces that feel considered at 60fps and at 300ms, not just in a static comp.
