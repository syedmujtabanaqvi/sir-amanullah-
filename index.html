Introduction

Hailing from Hijrat Colony, Karachi, Amanullah Memon is a highly accomplished Pakistani MMA fighter and coach. Despite starting work at a young age to support his family, he relentlessly pursued his passion for martial arts. His hard work paid off, making him the first fighter from Karachi to win an MMA title in Islamabad, securing a Gold Medal at the Thailand World Martial Arts Summit, and earning the Asian Pacific Award. Currently, he serves as the Head Coach at 'Fighter's Den,' training the next generation of fighters, and is actively seeking sponsors to represent Pakistan on the global stage.
