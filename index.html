The Fighter 01 / ABOUT

Built in Hijrat Colony

First fighter from Karachi to win an MMA title in Islamabad — now training the next generation.

Hailing from Hijrat Colony, Karachi, Amanullah Memon is a highly accomplished Pakistani MMA fighter and coach. Despite starting work at a young age to support his family, he relentlessly pursued his passion for martial arts.

His hard work paid off: a Gold Medal at the Thailand World Martial Arts Summit, an Asian Pacific Award, and the distinction of being the first fighter from Karachi to win an MMA title in Islamabad. He now serves as Head Coach at Fighter's Den, and is actively seeking sponsors to represent Pakistan on the global stage.
