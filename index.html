Introduction

Amanullah Memon is from Hijrat colony, Karachi, Pakistan. When he was only 15, he started attending different conferences, workshops, seminars and other events that helped him learn various life skills. He belongs to an underprivileged family, where his father is a daily wage earner. From a very young age, he started working at a local hotel to financially support his family. While continuing to work, he also focused on his education. During his spare time, he started to participate in MMA and BJJ along with some locals of his area. With time, he developed a passion for the sport and decided to pursue it professionally
